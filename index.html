FAVORITES / 02

Games I Like

01

Assassin's Creed Origins

ACTION RPG PC
Why I Like This:

Origins basically reignited my love for the series — ancient Egypt is just an insane setting to explore, and Bayek might be one of the most genuinely likable protagonists in the whole franchise. It's the kind of game where I'd lose hours just wandering around with no quest in mind.

02

Death Stranding 1 & 2

ACTION-ADVENTURE PC
Why I Like This:

Most people wrote it off as a walking simulator, but I actually love how meditative it feels — there's something oddly peaceful about traversing a broken world and leaving things behind for strangers. The story is weird in the best way, and it stuck with me long after the credits rolled.

03

Black Myth: Wukong

ACTION RPG RIOT
Why I Like This:

It's brutally hard but never feels unfair, and every boss fight feels like an actual event. The world they built around the Monkey King legend is gorgeous and surprisingly deep if you actually pay attention to the lore.

04

Red Dead Redemption 2

OPEN WORLD PC
Why I Like This:

Honestly, it's less of a game and more of an experience — I've never felt this attached to a fictional world and its characters before. Arthur Morgan's story is one of the best written in gaming, and I genuinely took my time with it because I didn't want it to end.

05

God of War (2018 & Onwards)

ACTION ADVENTURE EA
Why I Like This:

There's something about Kratos's journey that just hits different — watching this rage-filled god slowly learn to be a father is genuinely moving. The combat feels brutal and satisfying, but it's the quiet moments between Kratos and Atreus that keep me coming back.

06

Metro Exodus

FPS PSN
Why I Like This:

The atmosphere in this game is unmatched — it makes the post-apocalypse feel lonely and human at the same time. Artyom's silent journey across a dying Russia has this strange emotional weight that most AAA games never even attempt.
